* The BSP adopted the Balance of Payments, 6th edition (BPM6) compilation framework effective 22 March 2013 with the release of the full -year 2012 and revised 2011 BOP statistics. On 21 March 2014, the BSP released the BPM6-based series from 2005-2013. In BPM6, net FDI flows refer to non-residents' net equity capital (i.e., placements less withdrawals) +  reinvestment of earnings + debt instruments (i.e., net intercompany borrowings).
