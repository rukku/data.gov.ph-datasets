Note: Personal remittances is computed as the sum of net compensation of employees (i.e., gross earnings of overseas Filipino (OF) workers
with work contracts of less than one year, including all sea-based workers, less taxes, social contributions, and transportation and travel 
expenditures in their host countries), personal transfers (i.e., all current transfers in cash or in kind by OF workers with work contracts of one 
year or more as well as other household-to-household transfers between Filipinos who have migrated abroad and their families in the Philippines) 
and capital transfers between households (i.e., the provision of resources for capital formation purposes, such as for construction of residential
houses, between resident and non-resident households without anything of economic value being supplied in return).
